Sign Up

Description: Users create an account by providing basic information including name, email, and password. Email verification is required for security.

Security: Two-factor authentication setup is encouraged during registration.

Next Step: Choose user type (Startup or Investor)

Startup Profile Creation

Description: Startups fill out comprehensive profile including company name, industry, stage, team information, and business model.

Key Fields: Company description, funding needs, use of funds, current traction, team bios

Next Step: Upload pitch deck and supporting documents

Investor Profile Creation

Description: Investors set up profile with investment preferences, expertise areas, and ticket sizes.

Key Fields: Investment focus, preferred industries, stage preferences, geographic focus, investment range

Next Step: Set detailed investment criteria for matching

Upload Pitch Deck

Description: Startups upload pitch deck, financial projections, and other supporting materials.

Security: All documents are encrypted with 256-bit AES encryption. Access controls can be set per document.

Next Step: System matches startup with relevant investors

Set Investment Criteria

Description: Investors define specific criteria for matching algorithm including industry filters, funding stage, and deal size.

AI Matching: Smart algorithm matches investors with relevant opportunities based on criteria

Next Step: Discover matched startup opportunities

Get Matched with Investors

Description: AI-powered matching engine connects startups with investors based on compatibility scores.

Factors: Industry alignment, stage fit, funding needs, investor preferences, geographic location

Next Step: Schedule pitch sessions with interested investors

Discover Startups

Description: Investors browse curated feed of startups matching their criteria. Can filter, search, and add to watchlist.

Actions: Request details, schedule pitch, add to watchlist, pass on opportunity

Next Step: Request pitch session with promising startups

Live Pitch Session

Description: Real-time video conferencing session where startups present to investors. Includes pitch deck viewer, Q&A chat, and private notes.

Features: HD video, screen sharing, pitch deck navigation, investor reaction buttons, recording capability

Security: End-to-end encrypted video sessions with access controls

Next Step: If interested, move to deal negotiation

Deal Negotiation

Description: Collaborative workspace for negotiating deal terms. Includes conversation thread, term sheet editor, and version control.

Key Terms: Valuation, equity percentage, investment amount, board seats, protective provisions

Features: Real-time collaboration, comment threads, offer history timeline

Next Step: Once terms agreed, proceed to due diligence

Due Diligence

Description: Investors review detailed financial, legal, and operational documents. Secure document sharing with granular permissions.

Documents: Financial statements, cap table, contracts, IP documentation, compliance records

Security: Complete audit trail of document access, permission-based sharing

Next Step: Prepare legal documents for signing

Legal Documents

Description: Platform generates or allows upload of legal investment documents including term sheets, SAFEs, or equity agreements.

Templates: Pre-approved templates or custom document upload

Review: Both parties and legal counsel can review and comment

Next Step: E-signature workflow

E-Signature

Description: Legally binding electronic signatures on investment documents. Compliant with ESIGN Act and eIDAS regulations.

Process: Document routing, signature placement, identity verification, email notifications

Security: Tamper-proof signatures, complete audit trail, certificate of completion

Next Step: Deal is complete, moves to portfolio

Deal Complete

Description: Investment is finalized. All parties receive confirmation and executed documents.

Notifications: Email confirmations, document repository updates, portfolio additions

Next Step: Ongoing portfolio management and reporting

Portfolio Management

Description: Post-investment tracking and reporting. Investors monitor company progress, startups provide updates.

Features: Performance metrics, financial reporting, milestone tracking, communication tools

Access: Both investors and startups have dashboards for portfolio oversight

Click on any step to see detailed information
